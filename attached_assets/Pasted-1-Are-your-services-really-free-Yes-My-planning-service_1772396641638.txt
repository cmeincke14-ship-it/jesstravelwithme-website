1. Are your services really free?
Yes! My planning services are completely complimentary. I earn commissions from hotels, resorts, cruise lines, and tour operators when I book travel for my clients. You pay the same rate (or often better) than booking directly, and there's no fee for my time, expertise, or support.

2. How does the planning process work?
It's simple! First, reach out for a free consultation where we'll discuss your dream destination, dates, budget, and travel style. I'll then research and create a personalized proposal with flight options, accommodations, activities, and pricing. We'll refine it together until it's perfect, then I'll handle all the bookings. You'll receive confirmations and travel documents, plus I'm available for support before, during, and after your trip.

3. Can I book my own flights using points or miles?
Absolutely! Many clients prefer to use their own credit card points or airline miles for flights. Just let me know, and I'll plan everything else around your flight schedule. You can also book flights independently and I'll coordinate the rest of your trip seamlessly.

4. Can you book my trip using my credit card rewards or travel points?
While I can't directly access your personal credit card portal or points account, I'm happy to guide you through using them! You can book certain elements (like flights or hotels) with your points, and I'll handle the remaining arrangements. We'll work together to maximize your rewards while creating your perfect itinerary.

5. Do you offer travel insurance?
Yes! I strongly recommend travel insurance and can help you purchase coverage that fits your trip. I'll explain your options, what's covered, and help you choose the right policy for peace of mind. Insurance protects your investment against unexpected cancellations, medical emergencies, and travel disruptions.

6. What destinations do you specialize in?
I plan trips worldwide! My specialties include European getaways, Caribbean all-inclusive resorts, luxury cruises, Disney vacations, South Pacific escapes, and river cruises. Whether you're dreaming of Italy, Bora Bora, or a family cruise—I've got you covered.

7. How far in advance should I book my trip?
It depends on your destination! For popular times like summer in Europe or holiday cruises, I recommend 6-12 months ahead. All-inclusive resorts and domestic trips can often be booked 3-6 months out. Last-minute trips are possible too—just reach out and I'll work my magic!

8. What if something goes wrong during my trip?
I'm here for you! If anything comes up—flight delays, hotel issues, weather disruptions—just message me and I'll work directly with suppliers to resolve it quickly. You won't be stuck on hold with a call center. That's the value of having a dedicated travel agent in your corner.

9. Do you charge cancellation or change fees?
I don't charge fees for changes or cancellations, but suppliers (airlines, hotels, cruise lines) may have their own policies and fees. I'll always explain these upfront when booking and help you navigate any changes if your plans shift. That's another reason travel insurance is so valuable!

10. Can you plan trips for large groups or special occasions?
Absolutely! I love planning group travel—family reunions, destination weddings, milestone birthdays, friend getaways, and corporate retreats. I'll coordinate everyone's needs, handle group bookings, and make sure the celebration is stress-free and unforgettable.

Easy! Just click "Plan Your Adventure" and reach out via email or the contact form. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your travel dreams, and I'll take it from there. No commitment required—let's just start the conversation!

Personalized service, expert knowledge, and someone in your corner! I save you hours of research, get you insider perks and upgrades, handle all the details, and provide support if anything goes wrong. Plus, my services are free—you get professional planning at no extra cost. It's like having a travel best friend who does this for a living!
