JessTravelWithMe.com - Website Summary
Purpose
Personal travel agent website for Jess, emphasizing her as the sole dedicated agent who provides personalized, stress-free travel planning services. The site showcases destinations, builds trust through testimonials, and drives conversions through strategic CTAs.

Design Philosophy
* Mobile-First: Optimized for smartphone users
* Personal Touch: Single-agent focus, not a corporate travel agency
* Clean & Sophisticated: Professional but approachable
* Conversion-Focused: Clear paths to contact and engagement

Color Palette
* Primary: #365b6d (Deep Teal) - Main brand color, headers, navigation
* Secondary: #f2f1ec (Warm Cream) - Backgrounds, contrast sections
* Accent: #d4a574 (Warm Gold) - CTAs, highlights, hover states
* Supporting: Whites and darker shades for text contrast

Key User Flows
Primary Flow (Destination Inquiry):
1. Land on homepage → See hero with Jess’s photo & bio
2. Scroll to “Where is your next adventure?”
3. Click destination pill (e.g., “Italy”)
4. Read destination details & testimonial
5. Click “Plan Your Adventure” CTA
6. Navigate to contact/planning page
Secondary Flow (Learn More):
1. Land on homepage
2. Open hamburger menu
3. Navigate to “About Us”
4. Learn about Jess’s approach
5. Return to homepage or go to “Plan Your Adventure”
Trust-Building Flow:
1. Scroll through homepage
2. Read benefit checklist
3. View rotating testimonials
4. See social media content
5. Feel confident → Click destination or CTA
